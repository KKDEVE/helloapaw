About Us

We provide care, shelter, and support to animals in need.

To raise funds for the project, HelloApaw organizes joint fundraising campaigns, along with launching an initiative promoting adoption instead of purchasing pets. The raised funds will be used to help city stray animals (cats/dogs). The staff will select, immunize, and sterilize these animals as part of the rescue process. Afterward, the animals will be sent to the HelloApaw adoption display center for a 30-day period of exhibition. During this time, potential adopters can interact with the stray animals face-to-face, aiming to increase their chances of finding a permanent home.

HellowApaw is a platform or organization that focuses on assisting stray animals. Additionally, they aim to raise awareness about the importance of scientific and civilized pet care in urban areas. HellowApaw also emphasizes the promotion of scientific and civilized pet care practices among urban pet owners. This includes educating pet owners about responsible pet ownership, proper nutrition, regular veterinary care, exercise, and socialization for pets. By spreading awareness and knowledge, they aim to improve the overall well-being of pets in urban environments.
